Description

Wang Yibo (Chinese: 王一博; pinyin: Wáng Yībó, born 5 August 1997) is a Chinese actor, dancer, singer, rapper, TV host, and professional motorcycle racer.
[1] He debuted as a member of the South Korean-Chinese boyband Uniq in 2014, holding positions as the main dancer and rapper of the group.
As an actor, he is best known for his roles in television series Love Actually (2017), Gank Your Heart (2019), The Untamed (2019), Legend of Fei (2020),
and Faith Makes Great (2021). Wang has been listed in the Forbes China Celebrity 100, where he ranked 71st in 2019,[2] 9th in 2020,[3] and 2nd in 2021.[4]
